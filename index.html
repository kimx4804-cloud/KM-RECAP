Data use notice & disclaimer

Source accountability

All indicators draw exclusively from third-party public datasets. Accuracy, completeness, and timeliness are the responsibility of the respective originating organizations. KOICA does not independently verify or guarantee the correctness of externally sourced data.

Intended use

This dashboard is a contextual monitoring tool to support situational awareness in FCV settings. It is not intended as the sole basis for operational or funding decisions. Triangulate with field reports and partner assessments before drawing programmatic conclusions.

Data currency & update lag

Update frequencies vary — near-real-time (ACLED, ReliefWeb, GDACS) to annual (World Bank WGI, ND-GAIN). Annual indicators may not reflect rapid on-the-ground changes in FCV contexts.

Coverage limitations

Small island states (Solomon Islands, São Tomé & Príncipe, Comoros) and conflict-affected states may have incomplete or absent data. Absence of data should not be interpreted as absence of risk.

Version note: Source APIs are subject to change without notice. Links to external sources are for reference only; KOICA does not endorse any third-party platform or guarantee continued availability.
